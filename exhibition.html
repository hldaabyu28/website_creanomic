Midfest

Midfest with the theme 'Bits of Joy' is a stunning art exhibition, depicting the beauty in every small detail of life. In this exhibition, you will explore the work of talented artists, depicting moments of happiness, serenity and magic hidden all around us.

Sapu Ajaib

-- Dodik Hendra

This art depicts a child who, who should be sweeping the terrace, instead plays happily with a broom like a circus performer. These memories highlight the simple joys of childhood that are so easy to find, in contrast to the complexity of adult life.

Masa Sekarang

-- Dodik Hendra

In contrast to the beautiful, carefree childhood, a new unwelcome companion emerges in the present: depression. Nights are haunted by a sleep thief that ruthlessly steals peace. The labyrinth of anxiety and uncertainty proves difficult to navigate, turning restful sleep into a distant dream.

Harmony Like Koi

-- Vichtiani Handayani

Depicting a peaceful blend of human interaction with nature, this piece features a woman with water and koi fish, symbolizing the delicate balance between humanity and the environment. It serves as a reminder of the existence of peaceful coexistence and shared understanding that can be achieved.

Harmoni Layangan Kecil di Langit Biru

-- Abidillah Luqman Akhtar

A reminiscence of childhood, where friends flew vibrant kites in the clear sky. Small smiles were etched while their kites danced freely like butterflies in the open sky. On bright, colorful days, laughter resonated, accompanied by the graceful shadows of kites gliding above the green grass.

Grateful

-- Arzeti Azizah Afif

Being grateful for what happened in childhood, including special moments of happiness




Fairytale

-- Anjani Audita

Fairytale reflects the character's childhood full of adventure and beautiful dreams. hearing fairy tales in childhood brings hope to children about the existence of a magical world that no one has ever touched, and small children always try to find it.

Eternal All Time

-- Rafidah Kusuma W.

I want you to enjoy this painting from me with heart, where at that time, there was only the laughter of happy children by the clear river and the coolness of the green hills without any sadness.

Bereksplorasi

-- Arzeti Azizah Afif

Exploring new things since childhood is truly enjoyable, especially when allowed to discover them on your own. It's a childhood moment of happiness that can never be repeated

Ayo AAK

-- Alivia Naila Izzah

When you're being fussy during meals, Mom usually swings the spoon like an airplane zooming by, saying, 'Come on, dear, the airplane is about to land.' Ah, indeed, Mom's bites taste more delicious, don't they?

Anak Digital

-- Tiazza Winneythou

This work reflects on how digital technology has influenced the lives of children. It portrays digital children created through games like Tamagotchi and Poo, which have become daily companions.

A Child Who Missed The Beauty Childhood

-- Faula Mulyani

The piece portrays a child longing for the lost beauty of childhood, expressing their escape into a world of imagination as a response to early trauma. The child creates a fantasy world, finding awe in its beauty.

T.O.P - Through Our Power

-- Karya Kelvin

Try, the only thing we can do, I know this sounds crazy. But this is true, this world will not support you at all, everything you do is just in vain if the effort you put in is not worth it.
