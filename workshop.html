Paint Your Scenery

The workshop with the theme "Paint Your Scenery" is an activity designed to equip participants with skills and knowledge in creating works of painting. Apart from being given material regarding painting, participants were also given the opportunity to compete in this activity to create the best work of painting.

Acrylic on Canvas 20 x 30
Presenter : Kamaliatul Muchsinah
Kit : Canvas, Paint, Brushes
Time : 2 hours
